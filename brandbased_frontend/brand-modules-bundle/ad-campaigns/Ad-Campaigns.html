Where Your Ads Appear

Choose where BrandBased serves AdSense placements for your brand.

Across BrandBased platform partners

Always on

Your brand's ads are automatically syndicated across the BrandBased platform partner network — partner storefronts, brand discovery surfaces, and integrated digital platforms — wherever BrandBased serves ad inventory.

On your own brand website

Optionally let BrandBased serve AdSense ad units on your own brand website. When enabled, BrandBased adds an ad slot to your site — you keep full control of placement, and you can opt out at any time.

Connecting Google AdSense

To start earning, brands need a Google AdSense account and must register the website they'd like monetised. Google reviews each site against the AdSense Program Policies before it's eligible to serve ads.

  1. 1

    Sign in to Google AdSense

    Use your existing AdSense account or create one at adsense.google.com, then connect it to BrandBased below.

  2. 2

    Register your brand website in AdSense

    Add the website you'd like monetised as a site in your AdSense account. Site verification is required before Google will serve ads on it through BrandBased.

  3. 3

    Pass content review

    AdSense requires sites to comply with the AdSense Program Policies. Original, high-quality content with clear ownership and a privacy policy is the minimum bar — adult, dangerous, deceptive, or copyright-infringing content will be rejected.

ⓘ

Content compatibility: Google AdSense reviews every brand website for content quality and policy compliance. If your site doesn't meet AdSense requirements, ads will only run across the BrandBased platform partner network until your site is approved.
